ABOUT

The project showcases a management system during the Covid-19 pandemic. It contains 5 entities- Patient seeking to take the covid test, Hospital at which the test will be conducted, Doctor who will be assigned to the patient, Covid test which is to be taken by the patient and the report generated from the results of the Covid test. The database system will keep track of patients who tested positive and hence admitted to the hospital and patients who tested negative. Afterwards, the database also keeps track of the patients who, after admission have recovered or have died due to the virus. A user will be able to enter the required input in order to access the details of specific patients. They will have access to the status of the patient as well as the report of the patient. The project is also used to keep a tally of the mortality rate and the number of deaths which have occurred due to the Covid 19 pandemic.
